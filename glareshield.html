EFIS Control Panel — Captain

FIN1411VU
P/NMA404572012825 (Schematics)
Captain's Electronic Flight Instrument System control panel. Contains BARO REF selector · CHRONO · FD · LS · CSTR · WPT · VORD · NDB · ARPT range and mode selections for PFD and ND. Also includes SIDESTICK PRIORITY indicator.

AFS — Auto Flight System (FCU)

FIN—
P/NC16455AB (Schematics)
Flight Control Unit — central autopilot and autothrust management panel. Contains SPD/MACH · HDG/TRK · ALT · V/S-FPA windows and knobs, AP1 · AP2 · A/THR · EXPED · APPR · LOC · ALT pushbuttons.

DISPLAY UNIT CONTROL PANEL

FIN1313VU
P/NMA404575012825 (Schematics)
Display Unit Control Panel — controls the brightness of the OIT, PFD, ND & MFD . It contains PFD/ND PB When pressed, it exchanges the onside PFD and the ND, regardless of their default position and a RECONF PB where it's used in case of display failure to reconfigure one of the remaining display units to recover the lost display.

EFIS Control Panel — First Officer

FIN1412VU
P/NMA404572022825 (Schematics)
First Officer's Electronic Flight Instrument System control panel. Mirror of Capt EFIS — contains BARO REF selector · CHRONO · FD · LS · CSTR · WPT · VORD · NDB · ARPT range and mode selections. Also includes SIDESTICK PRIORITY indicator (FO side).

Capt Loud Speaker Controller & Radio PTT

FIN1413VU
P/NMA404571012825 (Schematics)
Captain side loud speaker volume controller and Radio Push-To-Talk (PTT) switch panel. Controls audio output level to the cockpit speaker and enables radio transmission from the glareshield area.
