Stdby Compass & Eye Reference

Backup mechanical compass and pilot seat alignment indicators.

STDBY COMPASS
EYE REF INDICATOR
CALIBRATION CARD

STANDBY MAGNETIC COMPASS

TypeLiquid-Filled Mechanical
Provides a completely independent heading reference for the flight crew. It requires no electrical aircraft power to function, acting as the ultimate survival backup instrument.

EYE POSITION INDICATOR

FunctionSeat Alignment
Used by flight crews to correctly position their seat height and forward/aft distance. When the pilot aligns the **red ball** directly over the **gray ball**, their eyes are positioned precisely at the optimum design eye position for flight deck visibility and viewing instrumentation.

COMPASS CALIBRATION CARD

P/NN/A
S/NB52711512
Indicates the deviations of the magnetic compass. It maps the true magnetic direction (**FOR**) against what needs to be steered (**STEER**) to account for electromagnetic interference within the flight deck structure while the radio receivers are turned on.
