APU Panel

FIN1221VM
P/NXXX
Controls the Auxiliary Power Unit. Contains APU MASTER SW · APU START · AGENT fire extinguisher pushbutton. Also houses the ELT (Emergency Locator Transmitter) armed/test switch and Air Data Inertial Reference System ADR 1 · ADR 2 · ADR 3 · IR 1 · IR 2 · IR 3 selector knobs and ON BAT indicator. Alignment takes approximately 10 minutes. Also contains PROBE & WINDOW HEAT controls.

ADIRS Panel

FIN—
P/N—
Air Data Inertial Reference System. Contains ADR 1 · ADR 2 · ADR 3 · IR 1 · IR 2 · IR 3 selector knobs and ON BAT indicator. Alignment takes approximately 10 minutes. Also contains PROBE & WINDOW HEAT controls.

Capt F/CTL · EMER FUEL · EVAC · WIPER Panel

P/N—

ENG Fire Panel

FIN1245VM
P/N—
Contains fire handles and extinguisher controls for ENG 1 · ENG 2 · ENG 3 · ENG 4. Each engine has AGENT 1 & AGENT 2 discharge pushbuttons and a TEST button. APU fire handle also located in this panel.

GREEN & YELLOW HYD · FUEL Panel

P/N—
