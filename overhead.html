APU Fire Control Panel

Part No: P/N 380-APU-9921-A

Controls the auxiliary power unit fire extinguishing squibs and warning horn resets.

Note: Check status light matches lower ECAM page.

ADIRS Panel (Air Data Inertial Reference)

Part No: P/N 380-ADR-1104-B

Provides position, attitude, and air data reference computing to the primary flight data screens.

Note: Alignment takes approximately 10 minutes.

Hydraulic Power Management

Part No: P/N 380-HYD-5520-C

Monitors Green and Yellow engine-driven pump pressure architectures.

Note: Normal operating system pressure limits sit at 5000 PSI.

Main Electrical Control Panel

Part No: P/N 380-ELE-4400-X

Manages battery connectors, external power initialization feeds, and generator line contactors.

Note: Keep EXT PWR ON until engines take over loads.
