CVR Control Box

P/NS161-1390-01
S/N301469
Cockpit Voice Recorder Control Box. Contains TEST · ERASE · GND CTL pushbuttons. L3COM unit. Records last 2 hours of cockpit audio on 4 channels.

Speed Brake Lever

P/N321200-1M00
S/NBA00020
Spoiler/Speed Brake control lever. Deploys flight spoilers for speed reduction and roll augmentation. Positions: RET · ARM · FLIGHT DETENT · FULL. SARMA unit.

P/N965-1695-001
S/NISCA-000231
Contains TCAS traffic alert and collision avoidance controls, WXR (Weather Radar) tilt/gain/mode controls — CTL PNL-AESS by Honeywell Electro. Also includes PANEL and lateral trim controls.

P/N80835-00-02
S/N80835000000044
Flap and Slat Control Lever. Selects flap/slat configuration. Positions: 0 · 1 · 1+F · 2 · 3 · FULL. Diehl Av unit.

P/N058-002-003
S/NH002093
Parking Brake control switch. Applies hydraulic pressure to all main gear brakes for ground parking. Also includes ACCU PRESSURE · BRAKE TEMP · BRAKES indicators. IEC unit.
